# Aruba Arriba

Perfect summer time drank

----

# Ingredients

1/2 oz - Vodka
1/2 oz - Rum (White or Dark)
1/4 oz - Cointreu or Triple Sec or Grand Marnier
1 oz - Creme de Banana
5 oz - Fruit Punch

Fruit Punch Recipe
3 oz - Orange Juice
2 oz - Pineapple Juice
1/2 oz - lemon juice
1/4 oz - Grenadine
